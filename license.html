Exchange Policy Renabelle's exchange policy allows you to exchange any Renabelle product purchased online within 15 days of receipt, free of charge. Simply ensure the product is unused and maintain its original condition, tags, and packaging. Exchanges are subject to stock availability. To initiate an exchange, contact our Customer Services Department at renabellecustomerservice@beximtex.com. If you opt for an exchange, return the full parcel to our online store and notify our customer service team. We'll promptly process your request and resend the full order. You can also exchange products at any Renabelle store.
Please note:
Products purchased on discounts/special offers cannot be exchanged or refunded. No exchanges for Pajama, Leggings, Dupatta or Scarves, Innerwear, Home products, and Accessories. Monetary refunds are not available.
