Code of Conduct

Introduction
At Renabelle, we are committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive, and safe environment for all our customers, employees, and partners. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for behavior and interactions within our community.

Respect and Dignity
We expect all individuals to treat each other with respect and dignity. Discrimination, harassment, and any form of abusive behavior will not be tolerated.

Inclusivity
We are committed to fostering an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome, regardless of race, gender, age, sexual orientation, disability, or any other characteristic.

Integrity
We expect all individuals to act with integrity and honesty in all interactions. This includes being truthful, transparent, and accountable for one's actions.

Professionalism
We expect all individuals to conduct themselves in a professional manner. This includes being punctual, reliable, and respectful in all communications and interactions.

Compliance with Laws and Policies
We expect all individuals to comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and company policies. This includes respecting intellectual property rights and maintaining confidentiality where required.

Reporting Violations
If you witness or experience any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, we encourage you to report it to us immediately. You can contact us at conduct@renabelle.com.

Consequences of Violations
Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or business relationships.

Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Code of Conduct, please contact us at conduct@renabelle.com.
