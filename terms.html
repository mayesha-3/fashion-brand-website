Delivery Policy
At YELLOW, we ensure your orders are delivered as quickly as possible. After placing your order, you’ll receive a confirmation email. Expect your delivery within 5 to 8 business days anywhere in Bangladesh, with no shipping fees. We start processing your order within 24 hours. If you need to cancel or change it, please contact our Customer Services Department right away at at yellowcustomerservice@beximtex.com. When your order arrives, you’ll have 5 minutes to try it on. If there’s any issue, reach out to Customer Services Department for an immediate return.
Please note:
Partial delivery/exchange are not allowed. No exchanges for discounted/special offer items. Monetary refunds are not available.
