Frequently Asked Questions  

    
        
On renabelle.com you can: 

Browse the latest collections for Women, Men, and Kids. 
Watch the Fashion Show and discover the Runway looks.
Access special previews, our advertising campaigns and exclusive content.
If you are looking for a specific item, you can search by keywords or product code. You can check 
availability of each item in your nearest Fendi store directly from the product page.

        



Q: How can I track my order?

     A: Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with the tracking 
     number and a link to track your package.




Q: What is your return policy?

     A: We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in their original 
     condition with all tags attached. Please visit our Returns page for more details.




Q: Do you offer international shipping?

     A: Yes, we ship to many countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times 
     vary depending on the destination. Please check our Shipping Information page 
     for more details.




Q: How can I contact customer service?

     A: You can contact our customer service team via email at support@renabelle.com 
     or by calling our toll-free number at 1-800-123-4567.




Q: Can I change or cancel my order?

     A: If you need to change or cancel your order, please contact us as soon as 
     possible. We will do our best to accommodate your request, but please note that 
     orders that have already been processed cannot be modified.
